Top 15 Vancouver Canucks Players by Goals in the Last 5 Seasons

(2021–2026)

Subject

This project focuses on ice hockey, specifically the Vancouver Canucks. Using data collected from public hockey analytics sources such as MoneyPuck or Kaggle, the project examines player performance, team trends, and key statistics over the past five seasons.

The dataset includes measures such as goals, assists, points, time on ice, save percentage, goals against average, special teams performance, and team outcomes like wins and losses. These variables help reveal patterns in offensive contribution, defensive effort, player usage, and overall team success.
